Places to Visit


Albert hall

Albert Hall is a historical building and museum in Jaipur, Rajasthan, known for its architectural beauty and showcasing of Rajasthani art and artifacts.

Amber Fort

Amber Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Jaipur, Rajasthan, known for its blend of Hindu and Rajput architecture and stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Birla Temple

Birla Temple is a Hindu temple located in Jaipur, known for its architectural beauty, it is built of white marble and dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Lord Shiva.

City Palace

City Palace, Jaipur is a palace complex in Jaipur, known for its blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture, and showcasing of Rajput royal heritage and artifacts.

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal is known for its unique architectural style and its many small windows, designed for royal ladies to watch street processions while remaining unseen.
